T1--Chinese and Western Music Theory and the applications

This is one of the humanity requirement courses for undergraduates in Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
I grade the assignments and final exam for the students.
I gave a guest lecture on the trend of AI music generation.
I wrote a chapter for the new textbook for AI music generation(In Chinese), which covers the most traditional rule-based music generation to statistic machine learning models like HMM, till deep neural networks, language models, and the popular Large Language Models. In addition to the introductions, I designed the exercises for undergraduate students major in Engeering to try the python scripts to generate their own musics.

T2--Sight-singing and ear-training

This is a class for Minor degree in Music for undergraduate students in Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
I have perfect pitch myself, but I always use the relative relationship to help the students to understand music.
I designed a couple of listening tests to make the students tell apart the "color" of music.
I applied Curwen hand signs to help students sing the scales accurately.

T3--Conductor for adult ameteurs

I was the conductor to make 24 untrained people perform a 4-part chorus work in two weeks.
I designed a couple of voice training games to keep them interested and understand the principals.
I encouraged them to sing out loud in front of the groups and arrange them in the balanced structure.
I led the groups get the first prize in their community competition.

T4--Tutor in High School Physics and Maths

I worked as a private tutor for a few high school students.
For each class I prepare a couple of basic questions, and then followup step by step.
I like to design real-world related applications for my students to search for open-ended answers.
I also focus on the foundations of my students, doing frequently quizs to check their learning.
